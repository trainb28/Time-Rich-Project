The reframe

Money you can make back. Time you can't.

I spent 20+ years trading my best hours for a paycheck and calling it the deal. Then I left corporate and found out leaving was never the answer. What I had lost was my time, and getting out did not hand it back.

Time is the one currency you never get more of. Corporate was just the most common thief. Plenty of people leave and still are not free. The work is taking your time back on purpose, and pointing it at what matters.

You do not have to quit your job to start. Wherever you are standing, the goal is the same. Take back your time.
